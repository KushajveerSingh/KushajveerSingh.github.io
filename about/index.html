About

Resume. I am active on fastai forums and pytorch forums.

Check KushajveerSingh/deep_learning repo for a collection of my work.

Deep Learning Researcher with interest in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. Have worked with GANs, style transfer, image-to-image translation, colorizing segmentation maps, object detection, classification. Currently trying to expand my knowledge in the field of Computer Vision by exploring new topics in the field and experimenting with the stuff. A fan of fastai courses and an active member of fastai community.

Currently, I am in the final year of my undergrad in B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Punjab Engineering College(PEC), Chandigarh, India. I shifted my focus to studying computer science around the end of first semester and artificial intelligence in the summer break after first year. I was also part of Skill India program where I participated in IT Software Solutions for Business in 2018.

I am a national level handball player having played handball for 5 years from class 8 to 12.
